Job Title: Marketing Manager

Brief Description: The Marketing Manager is responsible for developing and executing marketing strategies to promote our products and services.

Salary Range: $80,000 - $100,000 per year

Reports to: Director of Marketing

Key Responsibilities

  1. Develop and execute marketing strategies to promote products and services
  2. Create and manage marketing campaigns, including social media and email marketing

  4. Collaborate with sales team to ensure alignment of marketing and sales strategies
  5. Manage marketing budget and allocate resources effectively

Essential
  • Bachelor's degree in Marketing or related field
  • Minimum of 5 years of experience in marketing
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
Preferable
  • Master's degree in Marketing or related field
  • Experience in the technology industry
  • Experience with marketing automation tools
  • Experience with data analysis and reporting

Brief Description: The Web Developer will be responsible for designing, coding and modifying websites, from layout to function and according to a client's specifications.

Salary Range: $60,000 - $80,000 per year

Reports to: Senior Web Developer

Key Responsibilities

  • Design, develop, and maintain websites and web applications
  • Create website layouts/user interfaces using standard HTML/CSS practices
  • Integrate data from various back-end services and databases
  • Gather and refine specifications and requirements based on technical needs
  • Stay up-to-date with emerging trends and technologies

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field
  • Minimum of 3 years of experience in web development
  • Proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and other programming languages
  • Experience with web development frameworks such as React, Vue.js, or AngularJS
  • Experience with back-end development frameworks such as Node.js or Django
Preferable
  • Master's degree in Computer Science or related field
  • Experience with responsive design and mobile-first development
  • Experience with web design tools such as Adobe Photoshop or Sketch
  • Experience with version control systems such as Git

Position Description Reference Number: XYZ34

Job Title: Front-end Developer

A front-end developer is responsible for developing and maintaining the user-facing side of a website or application. This includes writing code to implement designs, optimizing page load times, and ensuring that the website or application is responsive and accessible on all devices.

Salary Range: $60,000 - $80,000 per year

Reports to: Director of Engineering

Key Responsibilities:

  • Write clean, well-structured HTML, CSS, and JavaScript code to implement designs
  • Optimize page load times and ensure that the website or application is responsive and accessible on all devices
  • Collaborate with designers, back-end developers, and other stakeholders to ensure that the website or application meets business requirements
  • Stay up-to-date with industry trends and best practices, and make recommendations for improvements to the website or application

Required Qualifications, Skills, Knowledge and Attributes:

Essential:
  • Bachelor's degree in computer science or a related field
  • 3+ years of experience in front-end development
  • Strong knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
  • Experience with responsive design and cross-browser compatibility
Preferable:
  • Experience with front-end frameworks such as React or Angular
  • Experience with back-end development and working with APIs
  • Experience with version control systems such as Git
