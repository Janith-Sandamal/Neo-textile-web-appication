About Ceylone Textile

We are the Largest Listed Textile business Since 1990 in Australia

We Provide The Best Textile Industry products

Our company operates on a sale or return basis, allowing our valued customers to benefit from our extensive range of products without incurring the risk of unsold inventory.

At Ceylone Textile, we are committed to delivering exceptional customer service and providing our clients with a diverse range of clothing products to meet their unique needs. We source our products from reliable and ethical suppliers, ensuring that our products are of the highest quality and comply with all relevant laws and regulations.

With an annual turnover of around $60M, CTS has established itself as a trusted and reliable supplier in the industry. We invest in innovation to continuously improve our products and services, and we build strong partnerships with our suppliers and stakeholders to foster mutually beneficial relationships.

We invite you to explore our website and discover our extensive range of clothing products, including women's wear, men's wear, children's wear, and accessories. We are committed to being a reliable and trustworthy partner for our customers, delivering superior quality clothing products that meet their needs and exceed their expectations.
Thank you for choosing Ceylon Textile Suppliers as your preferred supplier. We look forward to partnering with you and helping your business succeed.
