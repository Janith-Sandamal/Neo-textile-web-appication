About Textile

We are the Largest Listed Textile business Since 1990 in Australia and Newzelend

Welcome to Neo Textile , an Australian-owned organization based in Melbourne, dedicated to supplying high-quality clothing products to retail outlets in Australia and New Zealand. Our company operates on a sale or return basis, minimizing the risk of unsold inventory for our valued customers.

At Neo Textile, we are committed to delivering exceptional customer service and providing our clients with a diverse range of clothing products to meet their unique needs. We source our products from reliable and ethical suppliers, ensuring that our products are of the highest quality and comply with all relevant laws and regulations.

With an annual turnover of around $60M, Neo textile has established itself as a trusted and reliable supplier in the industry. We invest in innovation to continuously improve our products and services, and we build strong partnerships with our suppliers and stakeholders to foster mutually beneficial relationships.

Thank you for choosing Neo Textile as your preferred supplier. We look forward to partnering with you and helping your business succeed.
